### Curencies commands ###

- Add currency to referential database: make add_currency CURRENCY=EUR
- Remove currency from referential database: make remove_currency CURRENCY=EUR
- List currencies in database: make list_currencies
- Retrieve current FX rates (currency / EUR) : make get_rates


### Industries commands ###
An industry is always linked to a sector. Ex. : REIT / Retail where REIT is the sector and Retail the industry.

- Add industry to referential database: make add_industry SECTOR=REIT INDUSTRY=Retail
- Remove industry from referential database: make remove_industry SECTOR=REIT INDUSTRY=Retail
- List industries in database: make list_industries
